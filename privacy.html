Privacy Policy

CamoAuto — Last updated: April 13, 2026

Overview

CamoAuto is a personal automation tool used by an individual creator to manage and grow a print-on-demand store on Etsy. It automates tasks such as product research and listing creation using AI agents. This policy describes how data is handled in the course of that operation.

Personal Use Only

CamoAuto is operated solely by one individual for their own personal business use. It is not a commercial product, SaaS platform, or service offered to others.

What This Tool Does

CamoAuto automates the following tasks on behalf of its operator:

  • Researching product trends and niches for a print-on-demand store
  • Generating product titles, descriptions, and tags using AI
  • Creating and managing Etsy listings on the operator's own store

All actions are performed on behalf of and for the sole benefit of the individual operator.

Data Collection

CamoAuto does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal data belonging to any third party. No user-facing analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind is implemented in this tool.

API Credentials & Local Storage

Any API credentials used by CamoAuto — including keys for Etsy or AI services — are stored locally in a configuration file on the operator's own device. These credentials are never transmitted to any external server operated by this tool, and are used exclusively to authenticate requests to the respective third-party platforms.

Third-Party Services

CamoAuto interacts with third-party platforms as part of its normal operation. Each platform's own privacy policy governs how they handle data:

  • Etsy — for listing creation and store management. See Etsy's Privacy Policy.
  • AI service providers — for generating product copy. The applicable provider's privacy policy applies to any data sent to their API.

Contact

CamoAuto is operated by an individual creator. For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact the operator directly.
